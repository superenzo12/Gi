About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Beer GI, where passion for craft beer and delicious grilled food come together in a perfect fusion. Our restaurant is dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience with great ambiance, live music, and the best selection of beers.

Exclusive Collaboration with Guinness Beer

We are proud to announce that Beer GI is officially collaborating with Guinness Beer to bring you the finest stout experience. Enjoy the world-famous, creamy, and rich Guinness, available exclusively at our restaurant!

Craft Beer & Grilled Delights

Indulge in our carefully curated selection of craft beers paired with our mouthwatering grilled dishes. From juicy steaks to sizzling skewers, we have something for every taste.

Live Music Every Weekend

Join us every Friday for an exclusive unplugged performance featuring the best local talents. Enjoy great music, good vibes, and your favorite beer!
